SWIMMING POOL

A swimming pool is a structure designed to hold water for swimming and recreational activities, available in various types and size

Overview: Swimming pools can be either in-ground or above-ground and are constructed from materials such as concrete, fiberglass, or vinyl. They serve as a space for exercise, relaxation, and social gatherings, making them popular in residential, commercial, and public setting

Types of Swimming Pools:

In-Ground Pools: These are permanent installations, often made from concrete or fiberglass, and can be customized in shape and size. They are typically found in backyards, hotels, and recreational facilities.

Above-Ground Pools: These are usually less expensive and easier to install than in-ground pools. They are often made of metal or plastic and can be disassembled when not in use.
